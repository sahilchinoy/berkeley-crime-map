Crime at Cal

Visualizing crime reports at UC Berkeley

Here's the full map of UCPD crime alerts posted from 2009-2013. Grid tiles are colored based on how many incidents occurred in that area. Click an incident to view the type, date, and time. Drag the slider below to change the hours displayed, for example, adjusting the slider to 20 – 24 would show only crimes between 8 p.m. and midnight.
